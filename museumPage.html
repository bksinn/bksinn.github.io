BE PART OF THE COLUMBIA MUSEUM OF ART

All CMA Memberhips include the Follwing Benefits:

  • Free unlimited admission for one year.
  • Discouts on concert tickets, classes, and lectures.
  • Free admission to over 80 Museums across the Southeast.
  • Exclusive invitations to special events, including Exhibition Members' Opening Parties.
  • Member Appreciation Day-Several times a year, the museum offers exclusive members special savings in the gift shop, opportunities to bring a friend, and more.
  • 10% discount in the Museum Shop.
